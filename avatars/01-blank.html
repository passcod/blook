Brief 01 — Blank

An avatar for me. Requirements are that:

  • it is square, distinguishable at Twitter sizes (about 48 pixels);
  • an original is provided in a resolution of at least 512x512 pixels;
  • it is G-rated. I'm not fussy with what that means, but I'm going to use this everywhere;
  • it is in colour;
  • you do not require my likeness or details for this.

I'm just not particularly interested in an representation of myself, but more in whatever you can come up with. You decide. Do anything you want.

Occasionally, on request, I'll add a broad theme, or weigh in on alternatives, but I try to keep my influence minimal.
